The Pulley Machine

Two weights of varying masses are attached to the same string, seperated by a frictionless disk. With this setup, you can examine tension forces, potential energy and kinetic energy.


George Atwood

A system like this is called an Atwood's machine and was invented by George Atwood in 1784 as a laboratory experiment to verify the mechanical laws of motion with constant acceleration.
