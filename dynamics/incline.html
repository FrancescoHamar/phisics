Object on an inclined plain (Note: kinetic energy is not conserved when changing values)


Objects on inclined plains follow basic Newtonian dynamics, with the extra step of trigonometry. As labelled in the simulation, it is recommended to find the forces acting on the object relative to the inclined plain. Then the process becomes much simpler, and the formulas boil down to:

Fnetx = max

Fa + Fgx - Ff = max

Fa + mgsinθ - μmgcosθ = max
