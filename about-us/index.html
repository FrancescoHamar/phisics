What is this?

This is a platform to make teaching and learning physics easier. It is organized in the units of IB Physics HL, but is universal to all high school level physics curricula.




Who am I?

Hey! I am a high school student who loves physics and programming. You can find out more about me at my portfolio website, stasostyk.github.io
