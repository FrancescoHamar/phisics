What is this?

This is a platform to make teaching and learning physics easier. Physics is often daunting with long formulas and numbers, but can be exciting and understandable with interactive simulations.




Who made it?

This is an open source GitHub project created and maintained by Stas Ostyk. You can join the team by visiting the contribute page, or visit the project and all of it's contributors at https://github.com/stasostyk/phisics.
